Major Agricultural Crops

Wheat

Growth: Cool season crop, sow in Oct-Nov, harvest in April-May

Major Regions: Punjab, Haryana, Uttar Pradesh

Optimal Soil: Well-drained loamy soil with pH 5.5-6.5

Key Fertilizers: NPK (120:60:40 kg/ha), Zinc (5 kg/ha)

Water Needs: Requires 4-6 irrigations, critical at crown root initiation stage

Common Varieties: HD 2967, PBW 343, DBW 17

Rice

Growth: Kharif crop, requires standing water, grown in monsoon

Major Regions: West Bengal, Uttar Pradesh, Andhra Pradesh

Optimal Soil: Clayey loam soils that retain water

Key Fertilizers: Urea (100-150 kg/ha), DAP (40-50 kg/ha)

Water Needs: Requires continuous water logging (5-10 cm)

Common Varieties: Pusa Basmati 1509, IR 64, Swarna

Agricultural Machinery

Tractor

Essential for plowing, hauling and operating implements

Types: Utility, Row-crop, Orchard, Compact

Power: 20-120 HP

Cost: ₹3 lakh - ₹15 lakh

Top Brands: Mahindra, TAFE, Sonalika, John Deere

Combine Harvester

Performs harvesting, threshing and cleaning in one operation

Types: Self-propelled, Pull-type

Capacity: 1-5 acres/hour

Cost: ₹15 lakh - ₹40 lakh

Top Brands: Dasmesh, Preet, John Deere

Fertilizers & Soil Amendments

Urea (46-0-0)

Nitrogenous

Most widely used nitrogen fertilizer in agricultural soils

Composition:

46% Nitrogen

Application Rate:

50-150 kg/acre depending on crop

Best For:

Rice, Wheat, Maize, Cotton

Soil Types:

All except waterlogged conditions

Application Method:

Broadcast and incorporate before planting

Precautions:

Avoid direct contact with seeds

Government Agricultural Policies

National Agriculture Policy

Comprehensive framework for agricultural development with focus on sustainability, technology, and farmer welfare.

Key Objectives

  • 4% annual growth in agriculture sector
  • Efficient use of resources (water, soil, nutrients)
  • Promotion of organic farming practices
  • Development of agricultural infrastructure

Implementation Strategies

  • Soil Health Card Scheme
  • Neem Coated Urea
  • Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana
  • E-NAM electronic trading platform
